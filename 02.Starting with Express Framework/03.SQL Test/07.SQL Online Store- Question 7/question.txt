Employee Sales Performance Tracking
Scenario:

You are the Database Manager for an electronics retail company.
The company tracks its sales team’s performance through customer purchases.
Each employee can sell multiple products, and the system records which employee handled which sale.

You need to find employees whose total sales amount exceeds ₹50,000.

Tables
Employees Table

Stores information about employees.

EmployeeID | EmployeeName | Department | HireDate


Sales Table

Stores each sale transaction with the sale amount and employee ID.

SaleID | EmployeeID | SaleDate | SaleAmount



Sample Input

Employees Table

EmployeeID	EmployeeName	Department
1	Rahul Sharma	Electronics
2	Neha Verma	Home Appliances
3	Arjun Mehta	Computers

Sales Table

SaleID	EmployeeID	SaleDate	SaleAmount
1	1	2024-01-05	30000
2	2	2024-02-10	20000
3	1	2024-03-01	25000
4	3	2024-03-15	45000
5	2	2024-04-20	40000



Expected Output

EmployeeName	TotalSales
Rahul Sharma	55000
Neha Verma	60000
Arjun Mehta	45000 (Not shown because below ₹50,000)
